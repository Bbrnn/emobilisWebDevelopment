About Us

Symon Orchards is a family-owned agribusiness dedicated to providing fresh, high-quality farm products to our community. Our products include apples, borehole water, vegetables, and more. We take pride in sustainable farming practices and delivering the best to our customers.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between farms and families by offering fresh, organic, and responsibly sourced products.

Our Vision

To become a leading agribusiness, known for innovation, quality, and sustainability.

What We Do?

Providing Fresh Produce Every Single Day

Planting

We offer fresh produce such as Spinach, Sukuma Wiki, Bananas, Apples, and more.

We offer a variety of fresh produce such as Spinach, Sukuma Wiki, Special Sukuma Wiki, Bananas, Apples, and more. Our vegetables and fruits are grown with care, providing fresh and nutritious options for your meals.

Animal Keeping

Our farm raises goats, chickens, bees, and rabbits, providing quality farm products.

Our animals provide fresh milk, eggs, honey, and other by-products. We also utilize animal waste as organic fertilizer to nourish our farm.

Water Management

We ensure a consistent water source for our farm, ensuring the health of crops and animals.

We sell and distribute high-quality, saltless water sourced from underground reserves. Our water is carefully cleaned to ensure purity, providing fresh and safe water for all your needs.
